## Scope

This standard covers all work products produced by `@ob-accounting`, including:

- Job invoices (initial, progress, and final)
- Change-order financial components (pricing and authorization)
- Supplement math audits (Xactimate line-item reconciliation)
- ACV/RCV reconciliation summaries
- Financial close summaries (per-job)
- AR aging reviews
- Warranty registration records
- Depreciation recovery tracking atoms
- Vendor invoice credit memo request packets
- Product-catalog pricing discrepancy summaries

---

## Pass/Fail Checks

### Job invoice

- [ ] **ACCT-001:** Invoice date present and accurate (matches AccuLynx or QuickBooks issue date). — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-002:** `job_id` and `property_id` both present and resolved. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-003:** Line items match the approved scope atom (`approved_scope` atom for this job). No undocumented additions. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-004:** Any line items added after initial approval must have a corresponding signed change-order atom. Auditor verifies the chain. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-005:** Payment terms stated explicitly: due date, accepted payment methods, late-fee schedule if applicable. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-006:** Tax treatment correct per `config.accounting.sales_tax_jurisdiction`. If sales tax is applicable to materials (varies by state), it is itemized as a separate line — never embedded in the materials unit price. — CONDITIONAL (required in jurisdictions where materials are taxable)
- [ ] **ACCT-007:** Invoice math audited: subtotal + tax + any fees = total. Discrepancy > $0.01 fails. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-008:** Final invoice includes depreciation holdback line (insurance jobs): the RCV holdback amount, the ACV payment already received, and the net balance due. — CONDITIONAL (required on insurance-track jobs)
- [ ] **ACCT-009:** `trust_tier = "evidence"` until the homeowner signs or pays. `trust_tier` may be promoted to `instruction` by QC after payment is confirmed. — REQUIRED

### Change order (financial component)

- [ ] **ACCT-010:** Change-order pricing uses the same unit pricing basis as the original estimate (same markup rates, same labor rates). Any deviation requires an explicit exception documented on the change-order atom. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-011:** Change-order amount ≥ `config.accounting.change_order_minimum` (default: $100). Below this threshold, minor scope adjustments are rolled into the final invoice as a note; a separate change order is not generated. — CONDITIONAL
- [ ] **ACCT-012:** Change order references the specific ops-domain observation that triggered it (OPS-019 link: the reason and CompanyCam evidence). — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-013:** Change-order authorization field: `authorization_status = "pending" | "signed" | "rejected"`. A change order in `pending` status may not appear in the final invoice or financial close. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-014:** If the change order was triggered by a code requirement, the code citation from the ops change-order draft (OPS-021) is carried through to the financial record. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-015:** Signed change orders have `trust_tier = "instruction"` (human-confirmed). Unsigned change orders remain `evidence`. — REQUIRED

### Supplement math audit

- [ ] **ACCT-016:** For each supplemented line item: (a) insurer's amount, (b) supplemented amount, and (c) delta are all present and the math is correct (delta = b - a). — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-017:** Xactimate unit prices used in the supplement match the version and jurisdiction noted in the scope-of-loss summary. No mixing of different Xactimate database versions in a single supplement. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-018:** O&P calculation: O&P percentage applied matches `config.accounting.standard_op_percent` (default: 20% combined overhead and profit). Any deviation documented with reason. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-019:** Total supplement request equals the sum of the individual line-item deltas. Rounding tolerance: ±$1.00. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-020:** Any denial of a supplement line item by the insurer is recorded as an atom with the insurer's stated reason. Denials are not silently removed from the record. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-021:** ACV holdback amount tracked per line item (for RCV recovery tracking). — REQUIRED

### ACV/RCV reconciliation

- [ ] **ACCT-022:** ACV payment received from insurer recorded with date and check number (or EFT reference). — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-023:** RCV holdback amount reconciled: original insurer holdback minus any supplement increases or denials equals the final holdback figure. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-024:** Depreciation recovery certificate of completion submitted to insurer within `config.accounting.rcv_recovery_window_days` (default: 90 days from install completion). — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-025:** If RCV depreciation recovery is denied or not received within 90 days, an AR aging flag is created and routed to the AM. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-026:** Full reconciliation: ACV received + RCV holdback received + homeowner deductible = total approved claim value. Any discrepancy > $1.00 flagged for review. — REQUIRED

### Financial close summary (per job)

- [ ] **ACCT-027:** All change orders in `signed` status are included in final revenue. No unsigned change orders in final revenue. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-028:** Gross margin reported as: (final revenue - materials cost - direct labor - sub costs) / final revenue. Margin below `config.sales.margin_floor_percent` on a closed job triggers a QC notification (the job closed below the floor; QC needs to know why). — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-029:** Any insurance-track supplement items still pending settlement at close are recorded as contingent receivables — not in closed revenue. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-030:** QuickBooks reconciliation reference: the QB invoice number (or job number) is recorded on the close summary atom. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-031:** Manufacturer warranty registration confirmed (warranty_registration atom present with non-null `registration_date`). — REQUIRED

### AR aging review

- [ ] **ACCT-032:** Any balance aged > 30 days triggers a Conductor notification to the PM and AM. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-033:** Any balance aged > 60 days triggers escalation to the AM with a collections readiness checklist: Is the signed contract on file? Is the lien waiver position correct? Has the client been contacted at least twice in writing? — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-034:** AR aging review produced within 5 business days of month end. — REQUIRED

### Vendor invoice credit memo request

- [ ] **ACCT-035:** Credit memo request references exactly one vendor invoice. Multi-invoice requests fail. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-036:** Vendor, vendor branch, invoice number, invoice date, PE branch/price zone, and source document reference are present. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-037:** Every disputed line item includes product match reference, invoice SKU/description, approved product/catalog match, invoice UOM, negotiated price UOM, invoice quantity, invoice unit price, negotiated unit price, and expected credit amount. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-038:** Product match is instruction-grade or explicitly marked as pending human approval. Pending matches cannot be used to send a credit memo request externally. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-039:** Negotiated price agreement is instruction-grade, active for the invoice date, and linked to its source PDF/file. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-040:** UOM conversion is verified. If invoice UOM and agreement UOM differ, the conversion factor and converted math are shown. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-041:** Expected credit memo equals `(invoice unit price - negotiated unit price) × quantity` after approved UOM conversion. Discrepancy > $0.01 fails. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-042:** Retail benchmark prices from Home Depot or Lowe's are used only as comparison context and never as negotiated-price authority. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-043:** Draft email contains no more than the single invoice's discrepancy lines and does not include unrelated vendor/account/customer data. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-044:** Draft is routed internally to Lucinda in the dedicated accounting Slack channel for review; the agent does not send vendor-facing email. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-045:** Follow-up task exists with due date no more than 7 days after request send date, status, owner, and next action. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **ACCT-046:** Archive-only rule honored: records are never deleted; resolved/rejected/obsolete packets are archived with reason and timestamp. — REQUIRED

## Math verification requirement (all financial work products)

Every artifact produced by `@ob-accounting` that contains dollar amounts must pass automated math verification before Auditor passes it:

1. All arithmetic operations (sum, subtraction, percentage) are independently recalculated by Auditor.
2. Any discrepancy > $0.01 is an automatic fail, regardless of how small.
3. The math verification result is recorded as an atom linked to the financial artifact: `{verified_at, verified_by: "auditor", math_pass: true|false, discrepancies: []}`.

This check is not negotiable. Financial artifacts that cannot be independently verified by Auditor are held until recalculated.

## Escalation conditions

- Any invoice over `config.accounting.large_invoice_threshold` (default: $50,000) requires AM review before delivery.
- Any financial close summary showing negative gross margin requires AM review before the job is marked closed.
- Any AR balance aged > 90 days without a collections plan on file escalates to Chris directly.
- Any supplement denial from an insurer that represents > 25% of the total supplement value escalates to the AM for relationship management consideration.
- Any vendor invoice overcharge packet with expected credit memo total > $1,000 escalates to Chris in addition to Lucinda before external send.
- Any vendor with three or more overcharge packets in 30 days triggers a QC review of that vendor's negotiated agreement, product mapping, and invoice-ingestion path.
- Any credit memo request without response after 7 days triggers Conductor follow-up; after 21 days it escalates to Chris.
