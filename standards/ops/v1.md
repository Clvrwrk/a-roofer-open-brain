# Standard: Operations Work Products — v1

QC Owner: Quality Control agent
Effective date: 2026-05-29
Supersedes: none (initial version)
Auditor reference key: standards/ops/v1.md

---

## Scope

This standard covers all work products produced by `@ob-ops` and by the Capture agent in the operations domain, including:

- Daily log summaries
- Crew briefing documents
- Materials order atoms
- Change-order drafts (operations portion — cost portion governed by accounting standard)
- Close-out photo checklists and album completeness verifications
- Permit records
- Safety observation atoms
- Subcontractor dispatch records

---

## Pass/Fail Checks

### Daily log summary

- [ ] **OPS-001:** `property_id` present and resolved. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-002:** `job_id` present and resolved. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-003:** `crew` field lists at minimum a crew lead name or ID. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-004:** `work_completed` field is specific enough to reconstruct what was done (not "roofing work" — must include location on roof, materials used, approximate square footage if applicable). — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-005:** `materials_consumed` lists quantities and product names (or SKUs) for anything pulled from inventory. — CONDITIONAL (required if any materials were consumed that day)
- [ ] **OPS-006:** Any safety incident or near-miss is flagged with `safety_flag = true` and routed immediately to the AM — do not hold for end-of-day. — REQUIRED if a safety event occurred
- [ ] **OPS-007:** `trust_tier = "evidence"`. Daily logs are never `instruction` until a human confirms them. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-008:** `era_of_practice` populated if the log references a code, standard, or manufacturer requirement. — CONDITIONAL

### Crew briefing document

- [ ] **OPS-009:** Property address matches the job's `property_id` in the brain. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-010:** Approved scope summary is cited from the current `approved_scope` atom for this job (not from memory or a prior version). — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-011:** Any site-specific notes from prior property atoms (Historian retrieval) are labeled with their source and date: "From property history, [year]:" — REQUIRED if prior property atoms exist
- [ ] **OPS-012:** Safety requirements relevant to the job type are present (e.g. fall-protection requirements for any roof pitch ≥ 4:12; hot-work permit if torch-down involved). — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-013:** Permit reference included if `jurisdiction.permit_required_for_reroof = true` and permit has been issued. — CONDITIONAL

### Materials order

- [ ] **OPS-014:** Manufacturer and product line specified (e.g. "GAF Timberline HDZ, Charcoal, 30-yr" — not "shingles"). — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-015:** Quantities derived from EagleView measurement report or manual takeoff; source cited. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-016:** Waste factor documented (standard 10–15% for hips/valleys; higher for complex geometry with justification). — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-017:** Materials order matches approved scope (no upgrades or substitutions without a change-order atom). — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-018:** Delivery address matches job's `property_id` address. — REQUIRED

### Change-order draft (operations portion)

- [ ] **OPS-019:** Reason for change order is specific and observable: "Found two sheets of deteriorated decking at ridge line — see CompanyCam photo album [link], photos 14–17." Not "additional work required." — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-020:** Scope of additional work is described in measurable terms (linear feet, square feet, unit count). — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-021:** Any code trigger for the change order is cited with its source (e.g. "IRC 2021 §R905.2.7 requires replacement of decking where gap > 3/8 inch"). — CONDITIONAL (required if code mandates the additional work)
- [ ] **OPS-022:** Change order draft is NOT marked as approved until signed by the homeowner. `trust_tier` must remain `evidence` until signature is confirmed; `instruction` only after accounting confirms executed agreement. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-023:** `property_id` and `job_id` both present. — REQUIRED

### Close-out photo album verification

- [ ] **OPS-024:** All required photo categories present (see `debrief.config.example.yaml → required_photos`): overview (4 directions), shingle field, ridge, flashing, gutters, soffit/fascia, pipe boots. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-025:** Any pre-existing damage documented before work began has a corresponding "before" photo in the album. — CONDITIONAL (required if pre-existing damage was noted in inspection)
- [ ] **OPS-026:** Close-out album includes at minimum 1 photo of the clean, completed state (no debris, gutters clear). — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-027:** CompanyCam album link is resolved (not a broken URL). Auditor performs HEAD check. — REQUIRED

### Permit record

- [ ] **OPS-028:** Permit number recorded. — REQUIRED if permit required
- [ ] **OPS-029:** Issuing jurisdiction matches job's `jurisdiction_id`. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-030:** Permit issue date and expiration date recorded. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-031:** Final inspection status recorded once inspection completes. — CONDITIONAL (required when job advances to closed phase)
- [ ] **OPS-032:** `trust_tier = "instruction"` (permit is human-confirmed truth from the issuing authority). — REQUIRED

### Safety observation atom

- [ ] **OPS-033:** Safety atom flagged with `safety_flag = true`. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-034:** `original_practitioner` includes the person who made the observation. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-035:** Safety atom is NOT `cross_client_shareable` by default (`consent_flags.cross_client_shareable = false`). Safety observations are client-confidential unless the client explicitly consents to share. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-036:** If the observation involved an OSHA-recordable incident, the AM is notified immediately (Auditor escalates regardless of pass/fail on other checks). — REQUIRED

## Sequencing checks (cross-artifact)

These checks apply when Auditor evaluates a sequence of job artifacts together, not an individual atom.

- [ ] **OPS-SEQ-001:** Materials order does not predate a signed Contingency Agreement or approved scope. No materials ordered before the job is won. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-SEQ-002:** Job does not advance to `install` phase without a valid close-out permit IF `jurisdiction.permit_required_for_reroof = true`. — REQUIRED
- [ ] **OPS-SEQ-003:** Change orders do not appear in the financial close without a corresponding signed homeowner authorization. — REQUIRED

---

## Escalation conditions

Auditor escalates to Conductor (who notifies the AM) rather than auto-failing when:

- A safety-flag atom is found that has not been reported within 1 hour of the log timestamp.
- A permit record shows an expired permit on an active job.
- A close-out album is missing for a job that AccuLynx shows as `closed`.
- A materials order references a manufacturer not in `config.manufacturer_preference` without a documented exception.

Escalation does not block the job; it alerts the AM for human resolution.
