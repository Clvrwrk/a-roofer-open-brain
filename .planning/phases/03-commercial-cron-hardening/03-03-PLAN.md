<objective>
Alerting (SC2 second half, D-04/D-05/D-06/D-07). Two complementary layers per RESEARCH: (1) a SQL
check function on its own cron that detects the conditions the edge function CANNOT see after it exits
— stale watermark, delta over tolerance, unreconciled pg_net dispatch, and cron-never-fired — posting
to Slack via pg_net; (2) an edge-function-side `lib/alerts.ts` that captures in-run hard failures to
both Slack and Sentry (richest stack context) at the single choke point in index.ts.

Purpose: closes the "unreconciled/silent ingestion failure masking a real outage" threat — the whole
point of a self-babysitting commercial cron. Both layers are needed: SQL-side catches "run never
happened"; edge-side catches "run happened and threw".
Output: migration 176 (applied to prod), lib/alerts.ts + test, index.ts alert hook.
</objective>
