## Invoice roll-up and vendor terms decision

- The accounting database should use a roll-up style model: unique vendor invoices from all vendors
  roll up into the canonical Pro Exteriors invoice/audit layer.
- Vendor records need a terms section so due dates can be calculated when vendor APIs or invoice
  payloads do not provide explicit due dates. Terms should support default net days, cutoff/day-of-month
  rules, discount terms, and source/provenance.
